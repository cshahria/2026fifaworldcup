💬 Frequently Asked Questions

Bags must be clear plastic, vinyl, or PVC, and no larger than 12" x 6" x 12". Small clutches up to 4.5" x 6.5" are allowed without being clear. No outside food or drinks, no large umbrellas, no selfie sticks, no professional cameras with detachable lenses. Always check the official Levi's Stadium bag policy before your visit as rules may change.
The VTA Light Rail (Orange Line) drops you directly in front of the stadium at Great America Station. If coming from San Francisco or the Peninsula, take Caltrain to Mountain View station and transfer directly to the VTA Orange Line eastbound. Check bayareahostcommittee.com for locations and schedules.
Fan Operations volunteers help fans navigate the stadium — finding their seats, answering questions, making wayfinding announcements, and keeping the energy positive. Shifts typically start 2–3 hours before kickoff. It's one of the most guest-facing volunteer roles at the tournament.
Just bookmark this site — cshahria.github.io/2026fifaworldcup — and check back after each game day. A new entry gets added after each shift with photos and a write-up. No app, no login needed.
