Day 1
Monday, June 1, 2026 📍 Santa Clara Convention Center, CA
🎥 Gear haul — June 1

Accreditation Day — It's Official. I'm In.

It started with a drive up to the Santa Clara Convention Center and a parking spot on the upper deck — ordinary enough. But the moment I walked toward the main distribution center, something shifted. This was no longer just anticipation. This was real.

The check-in process felt exactly like airport security: passport out, scan, verify. There's something about that ritual — the small formal moment where a system confirms who you are — that makes the whole thing feel weighty. Two fellow volunteers guided me through with calm efficiency. Photography wasn't permitted in the accreditation hall, and I respected that completely. Some moments you hold in your head instead.

Then the doors opened to the uniform center. Trying on shirts in different sizes, finding the right pants, lacing up the Adidas shoes. It sounds mundane but it isn't — every item is a piece of something larger. I walked out with a full bag: four t-shirts, one long pant, one short, Adidas shoes, accreditation pass, transportation pass, and additional gear for the six games ahead.

I also met one fellow volunteer from the Fan Operations team. There are 87 of us. We'll be guiding fans through Levi's Stadium — wayfinding, seat assistance, announcements, keeping the energy good. I don't know my exact position yet, but training starts June 7th and the first real game — Switzerland vs. Qatar — kicks off June 13th. Shifts start 2–3 hours before kickoff, which means early mornings and long days. I can't wait.

Today felt like the starting gun. The gear is packed. The pass is real. Six games between now and July 1st.
